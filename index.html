About Us

Based in Sharjah, UAE, Health and Confidence Medical Storehouse LLC (SP) operates in full compliance with UAE Ministry of Health regulations and Good Distribution Practices (GDP). We partner with international manufacturers seeking reliable access to the regional healthcare market.

Our state-of-the-art warehousing facility ensures proper storage conditions for pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and nutraceuticals with complete temperature monitoring and inventory management systems.
